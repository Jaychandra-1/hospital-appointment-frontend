Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?
Go to the "Book" tab, select your preferred doctor, choose date and time, describe your symptoms, and complete the payment to confirm your appointment.
How can I view my medical reports?
Navigate to the "Records" tab where all your medical reports are listed. You can search, filter by category, view or download any report.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and all major UPI payment methods including GPay, PhonePe, and Paytm.
Can I cancel my appointment?
Yes, go to the "Tracking" tab where you can view your current appointment and use the "Cancel" button to cancel your appointment.
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings → Security → Change Password. Enter your current password and new password to update your credentials.
How can I contact support?
Go to Settings → Help & Support → Contact Support. You can choose from Email, Phone, Live Chat, or create a Support Ticket.
Where can I rate and review my doctors?
Go to the "Reviews" tab where you can see all your completed appointments and rate/review your doctors.
